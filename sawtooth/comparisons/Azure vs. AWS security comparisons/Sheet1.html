Additional NotesAWS is more mature, which can be an advantage and a disadvantage. One thing to note is the adaptability of AWS, AWS is quick to integrate with other technologies and is able to quickly adapt to needs of its consumers (hence the over 1800 services). AWS, however, was not designed for multiple accounts in one organization, this feature has been added along with others but making an organization friendly cloud when that was not the design is a process (as you see centralized logging was not available until Dec. 2019, a necessary compliance service that companies had to produce their own solutions until that point). That being said both Azure, AWS, and other cloud providers are fighting to prove that they have high security capacity.
